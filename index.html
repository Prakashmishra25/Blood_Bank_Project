Our Services

Admin

The admin has a responsibility to see if the user belongs to the region which is under them and constantly have to keep updates about enquiries from donors as well as patients. Once the administration sees to it, it transfers them to the blood bank.

Donor

The donor can donate blood through filling their valid information on this portal. Each donor can donate blood on a 3 month basis.The registered donors will firstly get notified through the contact details provided by them during registration for the requirement.

Patient

The patient can recieve blood donation of any blood type by requesting through this portal. The patient's request is thoroughly verified before approving any donation.You will also have an option to view your profile so as to look for the past history too
